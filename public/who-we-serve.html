FAQ

Is the price of Voolt Marketing the same for all industries?

No. Google and Meta fees depend on factors like keyword competitiveness, search volume, and industry demand. This means ad costs may differ based on your industry and location. However, our experts continuously test and optimize keywords to find the most cost-effective options, helping to minimize your ad spending while maximizing results.

Are there industry-specific templates available in Voolt?

No, our template is universally optimized for easy navigation and high ranking on Google. However, you can fully personalize your template to suit your company’s needs by changing colors, buttons, photos, and much more, allowing it to reflect your unique brand while maintaining top performance.

I didn’t find my industry listed when creating a website. What should I do?

Choose the closest industry to yours and customize your website accordingly. You can edit the text, images, and other elements to precisely match your industry and business needs.

How does Voolt ensure industry-relevant SEO optimization?

Our team of marketing experts, with over 30 years of experience, conducts daily tests to train our AI, which automatically optimizes your website and ads using the most effective SEO techniques. When you subscribe to Voolt Premium, SEO is created automatically for your site.

We limit the number of industries we work with to tailor Voolt’s tools specifically for each field, ensuring that our SEO strategies are highly relevant and effective.

Can I use Voolt for multiple businesses across different industries?

Yes. With one account, you can manage up to 3 websites, each dedicated to a different business. For each website, if you want to, you'll need to subscribe separately to Voolt Premium, Voolt Marketing, or purchase a Custom Domain. Additionally, you can control and monitor each website independently via your dashboard, making it easy to manage multiple businesses in one place.

What if my industry has unique advertising restrictions?

When we tailor our services to your industry, we thoroughly research all relevant regulations and restrictions. You can rest assured that our AI will automatically avoid any prohibited content, ensuring your ads comply with industry-specific guidelines while still achieving effective results.

Can I switch industries after setting up my website?

No. The optimizations we do involve testing and learning, which would be affected by switching industries. So, the only way is deleting your existing website and creating another. But don’t worry, our team can gladly help you with that!

Can Voolt accommodate industries with seasonal demand?

Absolutely! Voolt Marketing is an excellent tool for navigating seasonal industries. You can leverage it to maximize profits during high-demand periods or boost income during slower seasons. Our flexible approach ensures that your marketing efforts align with your business’s seasonal needs.

How do I know if Voolt is suitable for my industry?

Voolt is specifically designed for most trades professionals. If your industry falls under a trade profession, Voolt is likely a great fit for you. Our experts continually optimize Voolt’s features to help professionals across various fields acquire more leads.

So, when creating your website, if your industry is listed, you can be confident that Voolt is well-suited to meet your needs.

What industries does Voolt specialize in?

Most of trades industries. To see the full and most up-to-date list, simply start creating a new website and explore the options available in the industry selection menu.
