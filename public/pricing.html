FAQ

What payment methods are accepted for Voolt plans?

All payments for Voolt plans can be made using a credit and debit card.

How long does the Learning Phase last?

The Learning Phase typically lasts 7-14 days at the start of your campaign. During this time, your ads are sent to Google and Meta, where they gather data to determine what works best. Various placements, bids, and audiences are tested to optimize your ad spending and maximize your reach.

Is there a discount for annual payments on Voolt Premium?

Yes, Voolt Premium typically costs $14/month. However, if billed annually, it’s $80/year. We also offer a 3-month plan for $30.

How does the Voolt Marketing’s billing work?

You can choose to enable auto-billing, which automatically tops up your balance when it reaches the minimum threshold of $10. If auto-billing is turned off, we’ll send you a reminder when your balance hits the minimum threshold so you can fund your account manually.

What happens if my payment fails for Voolt Premium?

If your payment fails, we'll notify you, and you'll have 7 days to update your payment method. After that, if the payment is not approved, your website will be deactivated until the payment is made.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my Voolt Marketing plan at any time?

Yes, you can adjust your plan whenever you add funds to your balance by selecting a different budget tier. Note that if auto-billing is enabled, your balance will automatically be topped up with the last amount you selected.

Is there a refund policy if I cancel my plan early?

No, we do not offer refunds. If you cancel your plan early, it will remain active until the end of its current billing cycle.

Are there any hidden fees or contracts with Voolt plans?

No, there are no hidden fees or contracts. What you pay is what you see, and you can cancel at any time. However, please note that we do not offer refunds, so if you cancel your plan, it will remain active until the end of its current billing cycle.

Will I be notified when my subscription renews?

Yes, you will receive an email notification each time your subscription is renewed.
